About HappyData

HappyData is a comprehensive data visualization platform that helps users explore the relationships between economic development indicators and human well-being across the globe.

Our platform integrates data from the World Bank Indicators API and World Happiness Report to provide meaningful insights for students, researchers, and curious citizens interested in global development.
